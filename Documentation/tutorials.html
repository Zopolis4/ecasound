Ecasound Documentation - Tutorials, Articles and Other Such Resources

The entries are ordered in latest-update-first order. If you have material that you'd like to see on this page, mail me (Kai Vehmanen) about it.

Maintained Resources

  • Ecasound DJing Tips (en) - Tips for setting up a low-end DJing setup using ecasound, alsaplayer and JACK. Written by Janne Halttunen.
  • Ecasound Effects FAQ (en) - Questions and answers regarding ecasound effects. Maintained by Janne Halttunen.
  • Ecasound Usage Examples (en) - Simple examples of different ways to use ecasound.

Tutorials and Articles

  • A Poor Man's Guide to Home Recording (be/nl) - A home recording HOWTO that explains using ecasound for multitrack recoridng. By Koen Breugelmans.
  • Ecasound as a host for LADSPA effects (en) - Dave Phillips' article "Linux Audio Plug-Ins: A Look Into LADSPA" covers use of Ecasound as a LADSPA host.
  • Ecamegapedal as a guitar effect processor (en) - Dave Phillips' article at O'Reilly Network covers use of Ecamegapedal as a guitar effect processor.

General Level Tutorials

  • Introduction to Ecasound (en) - Nice introduction to Ecasound at the MandrakeForum website. Written by Janne Halttunen.
  • Ecasound Tutorial (de) - Ecasound tutorial written in German. Author not known.
  • Example of mixing with ecasound (en) - Weblog entry that describes mixing with ecasound. By Paul Ford.
  • Achieving Reliable Low Latency (en) - Dave Phillips' article at O'Reilly Network covers techniques for improving the real-time performance and reliability of Linux systems. Ecasound is used as one example application.
